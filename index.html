About

Hello, I'm Irfan, an XR Developer at Rebel Goods with a wealth of experience and a passion for pushing the boundaries of immersive technology. As a former XROS fellow, selected from a pool of 10,000 applicants and sponsored by Meta and FICCI, I played a pivotal role in the development of the Sneakerland App. My focus on the Avatar Customization System and AR Try-On features resulted in a project that I proudly open-sourced. Beyond my professional endeavors, I've ventured into freelance projects, showcasing my versatility in Product and Architectural visualization. With a keen interest in smartphone-based 6DoF XR Solutions, I'm actively researching and developing innovative techniques, from hand tracking interactions to using a PC mouse as a 6DoF Controller.



My XR journey began during my Btech studies, inspired by sci-fi books and iconic VR headsets like Oculus Rift, Vive, and Cardboard. This passion led to the creation of a simple DIY MR headset prototype. Along the way, I've garnered recognition in hackathons and game jams, winning awards for projects like "Human Anatomy - Immersive Learning Experience" and "PowAR Game." My commitment extends to contributing to XR communities such as Manomotion and Lightship ARDK, where I actively share insights and learnings.

Human Anatomy


I developed a Human Anatomy learning experience as part of the Game Jam organized by Lightship. The theme of the Jam is to create an Immersive Learning experience. I chose to develop a Human Anatomy learning experience and found Z-Anatomy Atlas online. I used Z-Anatomy 3d models, Lightship ARDK, and Unity3D to develop. I used both Touch input and also Lightship ARDk's Palm tracking feature for interacting with the menus and Anatomy systems.

ARt


We can Draw 3d line Meshes similar to the tilt brush in Mixed reality with a passthrough camera feed on smartphones. I used ARFoundation, Unity3d, a generic Bluetooth VR controller with a QR code to track the position, and a cardboard viewer to develop this experience. We can draw 3D line Meshes, Change color, increase / decrease the width of the 3d lines, and Undo/Redo the painted lines.

PowAR



Won first prize in ManoMotion Hand tracking SDK Pro Game Jam, May 2021. Developed "PowAR", an AR, Hand tracking game for android (WIP). Used ManoMotion SDK Pro, ARFoundation, ARCore XR Plugin, Unity 3D.

Six DoF MR on Smart Phone

I do not have access to any VR devices like Oculus, HTC Vive, Index, Mixed reality Head sets. So I developed a small prototype inspired from Star Wars Vader Immortal VR Game, for Android smartphones. A Cardboard VR Viewer with small open for camera is required. Used ARFoundation, ARCore for positional tracking, Image Tracking feature for controlling the Light saber. This is developed for educational and learning purpose only.

Smart Phone as MR 3DoF Controller for UI interactions



I developed a simple prototype of a smartphone-based 3DOF controller useful for Head Mounted Devices. Install the controller app on the smartphone used as the controller. Connects over WIFI. Rotate or move the device to move the pointer on the screen. Touch the screen to interact with the UI element. Used Unity3D to develop and Unity's Network system to communicate.
